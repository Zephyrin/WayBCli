An equipment is the one you put into your backpack for hikking or travelling. It contains some basics characteristics like weight or price for a specific size.
The default view, show all equipments you own or you wish.

Into the filter menu below , you can modify the equipment's list.

The first row allow you to filter on category or sub-category. A category is a type of equipments like Clothes or Sleeping. A sub-category is more specific than category. Into Clothes category you can distinguish Pants and T-Shirts. Depending on what you are looking for, you can specify which sub category you want to display.

Congratulation, you just install Way B. ! First thing to do is to create some category and sub-category. Some more step and you will be able to add equipments and play with it.
